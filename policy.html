1. Introduction

KeepSafe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use our Personal Digital Vault service.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture: KeepSafe is built on a zero-knowledge model. This means the contents of your vault — your files, notes, and passwords — are encrypted on your device before they ever reach our servers. We cannot read or access your vault data under any circumstances.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you use the site. KeepSafe uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and session tokens) to provide and improve our service.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
ks_session Essential Keeps you signed in to your vault Session
ks_csrf Essential Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks Session
ks_prefs Functional Remembers your UI preferences (theme, language) 1 year
ks_analytics Analytics Anonymised usage statistics to improve features 90 days
No Advertising Cookies: KeepSafe does not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not allow third-party ad networks to place cookies on our platform.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from signing in to your vault.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

For analytics cookies specifically, you can opt out via Account Settings → Privacy → Analytics at any time.

1. Our Security Commitment

Security is not a feature at KeepSafe — it is the foundation. We have designed our entire architecture around the principle that your data belongs to you and only you. This document outlines the technical and organisational measures we have implemented to protect it.

Security Philosophy: We build on the assumption that our servers may be breached. That is why your vault is encrypted before it leaves your device. A breach of our servers yields nothing meaningful to an attacker.

2. Encryption Standards

  • Vault data: AES-256-GCM encryption, performed client-side before upload
  • Encryption keys: Derived from your master password using PBKDF2 with 600,000 iterations
  • Data in transit: TLS 1.3 for all connections between your device and our servers
  • Passwords: Hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12) — we never store your password in plaintext
  • Two-Factor Auth: TOTP-based (RFC 6238) with backup recovery codes

3. Infrastructure Security

  • Hosted on AWS with SOC 2 Type II certified data centres
  • Multi-region replication for data durability and availability
  • Automated daily backups with 30-day retention, stored in a separate encrypted vault
  • Web Application Firewall (WAF) and DDoS protection via AWS Shield
  • Continuous vulnerability scanning and automated dependency patching
  • Strict employee access controls — no employee can access vault contents

4. Vulnerability Disclosure

We operate a responsible disclosure programme. If you discover a security vulnerability in KeepSafe, we ask that you report it to us privately before public disclosure so we can address it promptly.

Security Vulnerability Reports

Please include a description of the issue, steps to reproduce, and your contact information. We aim to respond within 48 hours and resolve critical issues within 7 days.
